About Baran's Bakery

Baran's Bakery is a home-based bakery in Kitchener-Waterloo, specializing in small-batch, handcrafted cookies and treats. We use only the finest ingredients and bake everything fresh to order. Our mission is to bring a little more sweetness and joy to your day!

We offer pickup and delivery within the KW area. Custom orders and special requests are always welcome!

Taste the difference of real, premium ingredients and a baker’s love in every bite.
